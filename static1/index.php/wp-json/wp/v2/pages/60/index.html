Wolum<\/a> is my music project. The name comes from a phrase a guy used to say when I played music in class. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

„Turn up the volume“<\/p>– Sadik<\/cite><\/blockquote>\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n

He always said „wolum“ instead of „volume“ and I liked the sound of it. Back then I played songs from Flume, RL Grime, Mr. Carmack and so on. I wanted to be part of the scene and deliver my own twist of the sound. So I started mixing songs and learning music theory.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
